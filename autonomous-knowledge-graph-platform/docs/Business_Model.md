# Business Model & Startup Potential

## Product Positioning

The platform combines enterprise search, knowledge graphs, AI reasoning, and BI dashboards for organizations that need source-backed decisions from scattered operational data.

## Target Customers

- Supply chain and procurement teams
- IT service management teams
- Customer success and support organizations
- Compliance and audit teams
- Enterprise strategy and operations teams

## SaaS Packaging

- Starter: document ingestion, graph visualization, semantic search
- Professional: AI assistant, scheduled ingestion, reports, integrations
- Enterprise: SSO, multi-tenant controls, custom deployment, compliance exports, dedicated graph infrastructure

## ROI Demonstration

The executive dashboard includes estimated manual hours saved and cost savings. In a real deployment, these calculations can be tied to document volume, analyst hourly cost, incident cost, and avoided supplier delays.
